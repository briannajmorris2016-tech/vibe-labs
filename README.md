## Overview

Welcome to **Vibe Coding in Power Platform**.

In this hands‑on lab, you will build a **working application using natural language prompts**. Instead of writing traditional code, you’ll describe what you want your application to do and **Power Apps + AI agents will generate the data model, user interface, and logic automatically**.

This approach — sometimes called **vibe coding** — enables teams to rapidly prototype and build applications without deep development expertise.

In this workshop, you’ll build an operational tool designed for **UN agencies** such as **WFP, IFAD, and FAO**:  

**A "Program Inquiry & Referral Hub"** that allows teams to:

- Capture incoming inquiries
- Categorize and triage requests
- Assign them to responsible personnel
- Track their status and resolution
- Visualize activity through dashboards

---

## What is Vibe Coding?

**Vibe coding** is a development paradigm where:

- You describe functionality **in natural language**
- AI generates the **app structure, UI, and logic**
- You iteratively refine through **follow‑up prompts**

Instead of manually building tables and screens, you can say something like:

> "Build me an app that tracks food distribution across field offices"

Power Apps will generate:

- Data tables
- Columns and relationships
- Forms for data entry
- Screens and navigation
- Starter dashboards

# Lab Scenario

You are a **program officer working at a UN agency**.

Your team receives inquiries from:

- NGOs
- Government ministries
- Partners
- Members of the public

These inquiries may involve:

- Food distribution schedules  
- Agricultural assistance requests  
- Program funding inquiries  
- Emergency coordination  
- Partnership and collaboration questions  

Currently these arrive through **email, phone, and informal channels**, which leads to:

- Lost requests
- Poor tracking
- Slow response times
- No reporting visibility

Your mission:

> **Build a centralized “Program Inquiry & Referral Hub” app** to capture, route, and manage inquiries using Power Apps Vibe.
